📖 关于本族谱 About This Genealogy

本互动家谱记录了胡氏家族数千年的世系传承——从华夏始祖黄帝轩辕氏（公元前2698年）开始，经虞舜帝，传至胡公满（胡氏得姓始祖），再经陈国二十五世君主，延至名门望族安定胡氏，最终经江西南昌迁入广东开平，创立莲溪村。您将看到自明朝弘治年间（约1504年）至今的完整世代记录——跨越34代人，约1,105年的历史。

家谱不仅是一份名单，更是一部家族的生命史。它记录了婚姻、子嗣、迁徙路线、祖墓位置、土地与祠产，以及祖先对后世子孙的殷切期望。每一代人的名字背后，都有故事；每一次迁徙背后，都有抉择；每一个标注"不详"的空白，都是一段失落的历史。

本网站由家谱卷轴照片数字化而来——从数十页泛黄的谱牒中逐字辨认，再经多位族人反复校对而成。我们将其开放给所有族人访问，希望大家共同维护、补充和传承这份珍贵的家族记忆。

This interactive genealogy documents the Hu clan lineage spanning thousands of years — from the Yellow Emperor (Xuanyuan, 2698 BCE) through Emperor Shun, to Duke Hu Gui Man (the surname's founding ancestor), through twenty-five rulers of the State of Chen, the renowned Anding Hu aristocratic clan, and finally the branch that migrated from Nanchang (Jiangxi) to Kaiping (Guangdong) and founded Lianxi Village. You will see complete generational records from the Ming dynasty Hongzhi era (circa 1504) to the present — spanning 34 generations over approximately 1,105 years.

A genealogy is more than a list of names — it is a living record of a family's life. It captures marriages, offspring, migration routes, ancestral tomb locations, land holdings, ancestral hall endowments, and the hopes that ancestors held for their descendants. Behind every name is a story. Behind every migration is a decision. Every blank marked "unknown" represents a chapter of history that was lost.

This website was created by digitizing family scroll photographs — transcribing characters one by one from aging genealogical records, then verified repeatedly by multiple clan members. We have opened it to all Hu descendants, so that together we can preserve, supplement, and pass on this precious family heritage.

📜 族源：胡氏得姓 Clan Origins

胡氏源流：中华民族胡姓，源出有虞氏。黄帝（轩辕氏）→ 昌意 → 颛顼 → 穷蝉 → 敬康 → 句望 → 桥牛 → 瞽瞍 → 虞舜（重华）。《史记》载"自黄帝至舜、禹，皆同姓而异其国号"，胡氏一脉即由此而来。

舜帝三十三世孙妫满（又称陈胡公、虞满），于西周初年（约公元前1046年）受封于陈（今河南淮阳），为陈国开国君主。周武王以长女太姬妻之，封地百里，以奉舜祀。《史记·陈杞世家》详载其事。妫满在位六十余年，因谥号"胡公"（《逸周书·谥法解》：保民耆艾曰胡，弥年寿考曰胡），其后裔遂以谥为姓，奉胡公满为胡氏得姓始祖。陈国传国二十五世，历西周至春秋，前后约五百余年。

胡氏得姓至今已有三千余年历史，繁衍为中华大姓。据2020年第七次全国人口普查统计，胡姓人口约1,650万，位列全国第13大姓。自得姓以来，胡氏名人辈出，以安定胡氏最为显赫——该家族自西汉至唐末，保持士族门阀地位近八百年，世称"天下胡氏出安定"。

Origin: The Hu surname descends from the Youyu lineage: Yellow Emperor (Xuanyuan) → Changyi → Zhuanxu → Qiongchan → Jingkang → Gouwang → Qiaoniu → Gusou → Yu Shun (Chonghua, Emperor Shun). The Records of the Grand Historian notes that "from the Yellow Emperor to Shun and Yu, all shared the same surname but different state names" — our Hu lineage arises from this ancient root.

Shun's 33rd-generation descendant, Gui Man (also known as Chen Hu Gong or Yu Man), was enfeoffed at the State of Chen (present-day Huaiyang, Henan) in the early Western Zhou (circa 1046 BCE), becoming its founding ruler. King Wu of Zhou wed his eldest daughter Tai Ji to him, granting him a fief of one hundred li to continue the sacrifices to Emperor Shun. His posthumous title "Hu Gong" (Duke Hu, denoting longevity and care for the people) became the origin of the Hu surname — making him the founding ancestor of all Hu descendants. The State of Chen lasted twenty-five rulers over five centuries.

The Hu surname has over 3,000 years of history and is now one of China's major surnames, with approximately 16.5 million bearers (13th largest nationwide, 2020 census). Throughout history, the Hu clan has produced countless luminaries — none more illustrious than the Anding Hu Clan, which maintained aristocratic status for nearly eight centuries from Western Han to the end of Tang, earning the saying: "All Hu clans trace their origins to Anding."

🏛️ 安定胡氏：八百年的望族 The Anding Hu Clan

郡望安定：安定胡氏，得名于安定郡（郡治在今宁夏固原及甘肃镇原一带）。安定郡始建于西汉，为胡氏最显赫的郡望。胡氏安定堂即源于此，至今海内外安定胡氏后裔仍悬挂"安定堂"匾额，以志不忘本源。

始祖胡城：安定胡氏始迁祖胡城（字允安），正直敢言，慷慨有气节。西汉景帝三年（公元前154年），吴王刘濞发动七国之乱，胡城随太尉周亚夫统兵平叛，《史记·吴王濞列传》载"天子乃遣太尉条侯周亚夫将三十六将军，往击吴楚"。胡城以军功封大中大夫，从长安迁居安定临泾（今甘肃镇原南），卒于汉武帝建元元年（前140年），享年六十七，娶妻东方氏，封安定郡夫人，葬于灵台岩宝山。从此，安定成为胡氏的根本之地。

鼎盛时期：自胡城十二世孙胡遵（曹魏车骑将军，才兼文武）以后二百余年，安定胡氏位至三公九卿及将军、太守者达数十人，并出了四位皇后和太后。胡遵之子胡奋为西晋镇南大将军、尚书左仆射，其女胡芳为晋武帝贵嫔。胡质、胡威、胡奕祖孙三代以"忠慎清贞"闻名于世。《三国志》载胡质"性沉实内察，不以其节检物，所在见思"，其子胡威亦以清廉著称，传下"淮海家声"的美名。

北魏煊赫：北朝时期，安定胡氏达到权力顶峰。胡国珍官至北魏司徒（三公之首），其女胡充华（宣武灵皇后）临朝摄政十三年。在南北朝三百余年里，安定胡氏先后有8人封王，5人封公爵，20余人为将军、太守、刺史、三公九卿，还出了2位皇太后、2位皇后、4位王妃，成长为当时最具影响力的超级望族之一。北齐时胡长仁任尚书令封陇东王，胡延之家族亦强势崛起。

千古流传：由汉至唐，安定胡氏保持门阀族望近八百年，世称"天下胡氏出安定"，又有民谣曰："天下胡姓出安定，安定胡姓出临泾。临泾胡姓哪里寻，皇后湾和胡家坪。"这是胡氏家族最辉煌的历史篇章，也是每一位胡氏子孙应当铭记的荣耀。

The Anding Commandery: The Anding Hu Clan takes its name from Anding Commandery (covering present-day Guyuan, Ningxia and Zhenyuan, Gansu). Established during the Western Han dynasty, Anding became the most prestigious ancestral seat of the Hu surname. The "Anding Hall" (安定堂) — still displayed by Hu descendants worldwide today — traces its origin here.

Founder Hu Cheng: The founding ancestor of the Anding branch was Hu Cheng (courtesy name Yun'an), a man of integrity and courage. In 154 BCE, during the Rebellion of the Seven States, Hu Cheng served under Grand Commandant Zhou Yafu to suppress the revolt. For his military merits, he was appointed Grand Master of the Palace and relocated from Chang'an to Linjing, Anding (southern Zhenyuan, Gansu). He died in 140 BCE at age 67 and was buried at Yanshan in Lingtai. From this point, Anding became the ancestral homeland of the Hu clan.

The Golden Age: From Hu Cheng's 12th-generation descendant Hu Zun (Cao Wei General of Chariots and Cavalry) onwards, the Anding Hu clan produced dozens of senior ministers, generals, and governors over two centuries, plus four empresses. Hu Zun's son Hu Fen became Grand General of the South and President of the Imperial Secretariat; his daughter Hu Fang became a noble consort of Emperor Wu of Jin. The three generations of Hu Zhi, Hu Wei, and Hu Yi were celebrated for their incorruptible integrity — the Records of the Three Kingdoms praises Hu Zhi for his "quiet depth and inner discernment."

Northern Wei Zenith: The Anding Hu Clan reached its apex during the Northern Dynasties. Hu Guozhen rose to Minister over the Masses, the highest civil office; his daughter Empress Dowager Hu (née Hu Chonghua) governed as regent for thirteen years. Across three centuries of the Southern and Northern Dynasties, the clan produced 8 ennobled kings, 5 dukes, over 20 generals and governors, 2 empress dowagers, 2 empresses, and 4 princess consorts — establishing it as one of the era's most powerful aristocratic families.

An Enduring Legacy: From Han through Tang, the Anding Hu Clan maintained its aristocratic standing for nearly 800 years. A folk saying captures this heritage: "All Hu surnames originate in Anding; Anding's Hu originate in Linjing. Where to find the Hu of Linjing? At Empress Bay and Hu Family Plain." This is the most glorious chapter of Hu clan history — a legacy every Hu descendant should remember with pride.

🛤️ 迁粤始末：从江西到广东 Migration to Guangdong

南迁之始：南宋宝佑二年（1254年），有祖先自江西南昌府南昌县麦山村宦游广东，任广州知府。寄居新会后，娶妾卢氏，生一子良甫，遂定居开平。这一支从此在岭南大地生根发芽。

创立莲溪：及至明代，胡寅（字朝瞻）于开平创立莲溪村，成为本支的奠基之祖。莲溪村位于开平百合镇，依山傍水，至今仍是胡氏族人的重要聚居地。自胡寅公以下，子孙繁衍，历明清两朝，共传34世，约1,105年。其间经历了明清鼎革、近代战乱、移民浪潮，胡氏族人始终守望相助，绵延不绝。

海外播迁：近代以来，本支族人逐步迁往马来西亚、澳大利亚、英国、美国等地。如今莲溪胡氏后人遍布全球，但无论身在何方，族谱始终是连接每一位胡氏子孙的精神纽带。

Southward Migration: In 1254 (Southern Song dynasty), an ancestor from Maishan Village, Nanchang, Jiangxi, travelled south to serve as magistrate of Guangzhou. Settling in Xinhui, he took Lady Lu as concubine and fathered a son Liangfu, establishing roots in Kaiping. From this point, the clan took root in the Lingnan region.

Founding Lianxi: By the Ming dynasty, Hu Yin (courtesy name Chaozhan) founded Lianxi (Lotus Stream) Village in Kaiping, becoming this branch's founding patriarch. Located in Baihe Town, Kaiping, Lianxi Village remains an important Hu settlement to this day. Descendants of Hu Yin flourished through the Ming and Qing dynasties — 34 generations spanning approximately 1,105 years — surviving dynastic transitions, modern wars, and waves of emigration.

Global Diaspora: In the modern era, branch descendants have migrated to Malaysia, Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States, and beyond. Today, Lianxi Hu descendants are spread across the globe — but wherever they are, the family genealogy remains the spiritual thread connecting every Hu descendant to their roots.

🧩 字辈如何运作 How It Works

字辈，又称派语、行第，是家族中用于区分辈分、命名排行的固定用字系统。每一代人在名字中嵌入一个特定的字，同辈者共用同一字，从而一眼可知长幼次序。胡氏字辈诗共二十字，划分五句，每句五字，构成完整的世代循环。从"公"字辈到"魁"字辈，每个字代表一代人，世代相承，不可逾越。

The generation poem (字辈诗) is a fixed sequence of characters used to name each generation within the clan. Every person in the same generation embeds the same character in their given name, making seniority and generational rank immediately clear at a glance. The Hu clan poem contains 20 characters arranged in four 5-character lines. Each character marks one generation, from "Gong" (公) through "Kui" (魁).

🎯 先祖寄意 The Ancestor\'s Intent

胡氏先祖选定这二十个字，不仅仅是为了区分辈分，更是将家族的价值观与期望嵌入子孙的名字之中。这首诗是一个编码在血脉中的价值宣言——祖先精心挑选每一个字，勾勒出一条完整的道德与抱负轨迹。

上半阕：公甫柱襄协·奕世永昌和

前五字（公、甫、柱、襄、协）致敬家族的奠基者与柱石。公指先祖，甫为起始，柱是栋梁，襄是扶助，协为和谐——这五字构成一个叙事：敬仰祖先开启的事业，作为家族柱石，互相扶持，维系和谐。

后五字（奕、世、永、昌、和）则表达了对家族未来的祝愿：奕是盛大辉煌，世是一代又一代，永是永远绵延，昌是繁荣昌盛，和是和平团结。这五字寄托了祖先最深切的期望——希望家族世代兴旺，永享和平与繁荣。

下半阕：维持忠厚道·连捷文物魁

这十字转向了对子孙的道德教诲与功名期许。维是维护坚守，持是持之以恒，忠是忠诚不二，厚是宽厚仁德，道是正道伦理——祖先要求后代坚守这些核心品德，走光明磊落的人生道路。

最后五字（连、捷、文、物、魁）表达了对学术成就的明确期望：连是接连不断，捷是胜利捷报，文是文化学术，物是实质成就，魁是名列榜首。这是祖先对后代在科举和学问上"连连中第，直取文魁"的殷切期许。

整体叙事弧线：尊崇先辈 → 和睦共处 → 绵延不绝 → 践行美德 → 以学成就非凡。每一个胡氏子孙的名字中都承载着这条从过去到未来的精神脉络。

The Hu ancestors chose these twenty characters not merely to mark generational rank, but to weave the clan\'s values and aspirations into the very names of their descendants. The poem is a manifesto of values encoded into the bloodline — each character deliberately chosen to trace a complete moral and aspirational arc.

First Half: 公甫柱襄协 · 奕世永昌和

The first five characters (公 Gong — ancestor, 甫 Fu — beginning, 柱 Zhu — pillar, 襄 Xiang — assist, 协 Xie — harmony) honour the foundation builders of the clan. Together they tell a story: revere the ancestral foundation, stand as pillars, support one another, and maintain harmony.

The next five characters (奕 Yi — grand, 世 Shi — generations, 永 Yong — eternal, 昌 Chang — prosperous, 和 He — peace) express the ancestor\'s deepest wishes for the family\'s future: may it flourish through endless generations in lasting prosperity and peace.

Second Half: 维持忠厚道 · 连捷文物魁

These ten characters shift to moral instruction and aspirations for scholarly achievement. 维 Wei (uphold), 持 Chi (sustain), 忠 Zhong (loyalty), 厚 Hou (generosity), 道 Dao (the virtuous way) — the ancestor instructs descendants to steadfastly uphold these core virtues and walk the righteous path.

The final five characters (连 Lian — successive, 捷 Jie — swift victory, 文 Wen — culture & letters, 物 Wu — substance & achievement, 魁 Kui — chief, the best) express a clear aspiration for scholarly and civil service excellence — to achieve repeated examination triumphs and become masters of letters, bringing honour to the family through learning.

The narrative arc: Honour the past → Live in harmony → Endure through generations → Practice virtue → Achieve greatness through learning. Every Hu descendant carries this arc within their name — a thread from the ancestral past into the future.

    迁粤始祖记 — The founding ancestor's migration to Guangdong

    居江西南昌府南昌县之麦山村。在宋宝佑二年（1254年），宦游广东知广州事，寄居新会，去城百余里地，在开平的梁都（即现在的开平百合镇）娶妾卢氏，生一子良甫。及暮年，有从弟均寿公至，与之归，而卢太姚之母子独恋故土，不肯行，躲之，遂居焉。厥后竟不知佑祖之殁，所及十余世，历明、迄清。至清康熙己酉年（1669年），朴生翁接江西回书，始知佑祖葬于麦山上名石潭龟形艮寅山坤申向。

    [Summary: The ancestor served as an official in Guangzhou in 1254 (Song Dynasty). He settled in Xinhui, took a concubine named Lady Lu in Kaiping, and had a son named Liangfu. His younger cousin came to escort him home, but Lady Lu refused to leave. Thus the branch settled in Guangdong. In 1669 (Qing Dynasty), Pusheng Weng received a letter from Jiangxi revealing that the ancestral tomb of Youzu was at Maishan, Shitan.]

    十一、家族源流概述

    【繁】 中华民族胡姓，源出有虞氏。黄帝（轩辕氏）→ 昌意 → 颛顼 → 穷蝉 → 敬康 → 句望 → 桥牛 → 瞽瞍 → 虞舜（重华）。舜帝三十三世孙妫满，于西周初年受封于陈（今河南淮阳），为陈国开国君主。因谥号"胡公"，其后裔遂以谥为姓，奉胡公满为胡氏得姓始祖。

    [EN] The Hu surname of China traces its origin to the Youyu clan. From the Yellow Emperor through Emperor Shun (9 generations), and thirty-three generations later to Gui Man, who was enfeoffed as Duke of Chen (present-day Huaiyang, Henan) in the early Western Zhou. His posthumous title "Duke Hu" became the source of the Hu surname.

    【繁】 陈国传国二十余世，历西周至春秋。胡氏后裔中重要一支，定居安定（今宁夏固原），形成"安定胡氏家族"，为胡姓重要郡望。西汉至十六国时期，安定胡氏世系绵延不绝。

    [EN] The State of Chen lasted over twenty generations through the Western Zhou and Spring and Autumn periods. A major branch of the Hu descendants settled in Anding (today's Guyuan, Ningxia), forming the prestigious "Anding Hu Clan."

    【繁】 宋元时期，胡氏世代编号延续。至明代，有胡寅（字朝瞻）自江西南昌府南昌县之麦山村宦游广东，寄居新会，后定居开平，创立莲溪村。其后世繁衍，历明、清两朝，共传34世，计约1,105年。

    [EN] During the Song and Yuan periods, the Hu generation numbering continued. In the Ming dynasty, Hu Yin (courtesy name Chaozhan) travelled from Maishan Village, Nanchang, Jiangxi to serve as an official in Guangdong. He settled in Kaiping and founded Lianxi Village. His descendants flourished through the Ming and Qing dynasties — 34 generations spanning approximately 1,105 years.

    【繁】 此谱记载了从黄帝至明清时期胡氏家族完整的世系传承，是研究胡氏家族历史的重要文献。

    [EN] This genealogy records the complete lineage of the Hu clan from the Yellow Emperor through the Ming and Qing dynasties — an important document for the study of Hu family history.
